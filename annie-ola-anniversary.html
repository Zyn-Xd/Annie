A Letter for You ✉️

Dear Annie,
I keep thinking about how it all started with a random "Hiiiiiiiiii" in a comment section. Of all the posts, of all the comments — you were there. You asked "Wanna be friends? 😆" and honestly, I don't think you knew what you were signing up for 😄

This past year has been something I never expected. We're in different cities, we've never been able to just be there the way some people can — but somehow that hasn't mattered. You've been present in every way that counts. Every call, every voice note, every 3am text when one of us couldn't sleep.

You make the distance feel small. Not invisible — I won't lie, it's hard sometimes — but small. Because you fill up all the space between us with so much warmth that I barely notice the kilometres.

Thank you for choosing to stay. For choosing me, over and over. Here's to year two, and every year after that — wherever we are in the world. 💛
Forever yours, Ola 🧡
